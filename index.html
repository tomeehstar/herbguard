⚠️
Monitor if on Warfarin or thyroid medication
Moringa may enhance the effect of blood-thinning medications. Inform your pharmacist or doctor if taking Warfarin, Coumadin, or thyroid replacement therapy before starting this supplement.
⚠️
Bitter Leaf + Antidiabetic Drugs
Vernonia amygdalina contains compounds that lower blood glucose and may amplify the effect of antidiabetic medications like Metformin. This can cause hypoglycaemia if not monitored.

What you should do
1
Do not stop your medication
Continue taking Metformin as prescribed. Never stop prescription medication without consulting your doctor first.
2
Monitor blood sugar closely
Check glucose levels more frequently if using Bitter Leaf. Watch for signs of hypoglycaemia: dizziness, sweating, confusion, shakiness.
3
Tell your pharmacist or doctor
Disclose all herbal products you are taking. Your doctor may adjust your Metformin dose or suggest safer alternatives.
4
Space out doses if continuing
If advised to continue, take Bitter Leaf at least 2 hours apart from your Metformin dose to reduce the additive effect.
